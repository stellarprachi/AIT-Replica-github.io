Events

TechnoSparx

Techfest is a celebration of the Technology, Ideas and Innovations. Technosparx is an initiative of AIT campus to nurture the budding developers with technical skills and competitiveness to prove dexterous edge over the others. Technosparx is the Finest Platform for the Ingenious minds to show their talents in technical field & come together to put their skills to test. It is an assortment of various technological activities and literary events to honor the student’s interests, competencies and technical skills. It aims to motivating students to come together and share their enthusiasm and love for Technology. It provides an opportunity to the students, wherein they can exhibit their technical competence, knowledge and skill sets required to be an effective developer. Every year in February AIT is organizing this State Level Technosparx event for students to showcase their hidden talent.

Navratri

The Navratri festival is known as or coincides with the Durga Puja.The celebration started by seeking the blessings from Goddess Durga to protect the Gianjyotians from evils..The dance form known as ras garba (also joined sometimes by dandiya, which uses small wooden sticks), comes from Lord Krishna's worship rather than Goddess worship.The basic idea to organise Navratri is to bring students from different departments together.AIT celebrated the Navratri festival by playing Dandiya and Garba at its campus

Cultural Fest

A cultural function adds to the flavor and taste of life and makes it worth-living.The Cultural Committee of AIT is committed for holistic development of students, by creating an environment where talents are nurtured every year. The committee conducted various programmes throughout the year and brings out the vibrant colours of extracurricular activities. That is why, every student looks forward to it with keen interest.Whole the function was echoed with clapping. Everybody enjoyed the cultural function and highly appreciated the organizers.
